## 1: Shopkeeper: Do you want anything, my dear?  {#ch-1}

Shopkeeper: Do you want anything, my dear?
Customer: Yes, I want some apples.
Shopkeeper: How much do you want?
Customer: A kilo, please. How much is it?
Shopkeeper: Rs. 50. Do you want anything else?
Customer: I want a dozen of bananas. How much are they?
Shopkeeper: Rs. 36, please.
Customer: How much is the total?
Shopkeeper: Its rupees 86.
Customer: Here you are.
Shopkeeper: Thank you.
